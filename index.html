About Us

About Us

Ashi Industries was established in 2014 in the Basai Industrial Area, Gurugram, with a vision to cater to both automotive and non-automotive customers. Our journey began with two power presses, focusing on supplying high-quality components to the automotive sector. Over the years, we have evolved and expanded our capabilities to meet the growing demands of our diverse clientele.

Today, Ashi Industries boasts state-of-the-art facilities for in-house manufacturing of tooling, jigs, and fixtures. Our precision engineering and manufacturing processes are backed by advanced machinery, including Vertical Machining Centers (VMC) and Wire Cut machines, enabling us to handle both mass production and prototype development with ease.

We specialize in the fabrication of industrial pallets and trolleys, offering customized solutions that cater to various industrial needs. Our commitment to innovation and excellence ensures that every product we manufacture meets the highest standards of quality and performance.

At Ashi Industries, we pride ourselves on our ability to deliver precise and varied types of tooling, adhering to stringent customer specifications. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional service and support, ensuring that each project is completed to the utmost satisfaction of our clients.

Our Mission

Our mission is to drive excellence in manufacturing by consistently meeting the diverse quality needs of our customers. At Ashi Industries, we are committed to ensuring that every component produced at our facility is of superior quality and adheres to customer specifications.

We strive to offer the best quality at the most feasible prices, prioritizing customer satisfaction as the cornerstone of our work strategy. Our mission is guided by the following principles:

  • Precision and Quality: We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of precision and quality in our manufacturing processes. Our advanced equipment and skilled workforce enable us to produce components and tooling that meet exacting specifications.
  • Customer-Centric Approach:Understanding and addressing the unique needs of our customers is at the heart of everything we do. We work closely with our clients to deliver tailored solutions that enhance their operational efficiency and productivity.
  • Innovation and Excellence:We continuously invest in new technologies and innovative practices to stay ahead in the industry. Our commitment to excellence drives us to improve our processes and products, ensuring we deliver the best possible outcomes for our customers.
  • Sustainability and Responsibility: We are committed to sustainable manufacturing practices that minimize our environmental impact. Our focus on responsible production ensures that we contribute positively to the community and the environment.

    • At Ashi Industries, our goal is to build lasting relationships with our customers by delivering products and services that exceed their expectations. We believe in the power of collaboration and are dedicated to fostering a culture of continuous improvement and mutual respect.

    At Ashi Industries, we don't just manufacture components; we engineer solutions that elevate standards, turning precision into a realized vision.
